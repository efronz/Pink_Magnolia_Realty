About Pink Magnolia Realty

Welcome to Pink Magnolia Realty, a boutique real estate brokerage proudly serving the Piedmont Triad of North Carolina. Whether you're finding your perfect home or maximizing your property's value, we are committed to delivering the best outcomes and ensuring you feel supported throughout every step of the journey.

Real estate is more than just a transaction, it's part of your life and your dreams, and we are truly honored to help make it a success. We believe that every client deserves a personalized experience tailored to their unique needs. That's why at Pink Magnolia Realty, we go above and beyond to ensure you feel informed, confident, and excited about your next move.

Serving the Piedmont Triad

From the vibrant city life of Greensboro and Winston-Salem to the scenic beauty of High Point and beyond, we are passionate about helping our clients find the right property in the Piedmont Triad.
No matter where you're looking to buy or sell in the Triad, we know the local market inside and out. We're here to help you navigate it with confidence.

Meet Sharon and Steven McGee


Pink Magnolia Realty is proudly owned by Sharon McGee and her son, Steven McGee, who bring a combined experience of nearly 15 years in the real estate industry. As a family-owned business, we approach each transaction with care, integrity, and a deep understanding of what it means to make your dreams a reality.

Lets make your next move together

Whether you're buying or selling, Pink Magnolia Realty is here to guide you through the process with honesty, care, and dedication. Reach out today!
